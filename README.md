# Lex — study & teaching companion

A single-file, offline-friendly web app for law students and teachers (India-focused).
No install, no server, no build step — it's one `index.html`.

**Live version:** see the GitHub Pages link in the repository's *About* section.

## Modules
- **Notes** — a rich notebook (Header / Module / Topic / Sub-topic structure) plus a colour **sticky board / whiteboard** for quick thoughts.
- **Knowledge base** — searchable, ready-made notes on Labour, Corporate, Constitutional and Contract law. Save any topic to your notes.
- **UGC-NET prep** — generate quizzes on any topic, take timed mocks, track weak areas, and a **cheat-sheet** of aptitude/quant formulae, teaching & research aptitude.
- **Question bank** — describe a paper in plain English and get a full question paper + model answer key for your students.
- **Resume builder** — multiple templates (academic, professor/HOD, corporate, company secretary, litigation, judicial, fresher), an ATS keyword check, upload-an-existing-resume AI autofill, and PDF/Word export.
- **My space** — a personal attendance calendar with a per-class salary calculator.

## Using the AI features
The AI parts (Ask Gemini, quiz/question generation, resume autofill) use **Google Gemini**.
The free tier needs no credit card:

1. Get a free key at <https://aistudio.google.com/apikey>
2. In the app, click **Gemini key** (top-right) and paste it.

Your key is stored only in your own browser and is sent directly to Google — nowhere else.
The manual features (notes, sticky board, calendar/salary, cheat sheets, resume templates,
reading the knowledge base) all work with **no key at all**.

## Running it yourself
Just download `index.html` and double-click it, or open the hosted link.
